Rath Yatra

Ratha Yatra or chariot festival, is any public procession in a chariot.[3][4] They are held annually during festivals in India, Nepal, and Sri Lanka.[5] The term also refers to the popular annual Ratha Yatra of Puri[6] that involves a public procession with a chariot with deities Jagannath (Vishnu avatar), Balabhadra (his brother), Subhadra (his sister), and Sudarshana Chakra (his weapon) on a ratha, which is a wooden deula-shaped chariot.[7][8]
