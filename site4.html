Barabati Fort

Barabati Fort is a 987 CE fort built by Marakata Keshari of Somavanshi (Keshari) dynasty in Cuttack, Odisha.[1] The ruins of the fort remain with its moat, gate, and the earthen mound of the nine-storied palace.
